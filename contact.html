Frequently Asked Questions

We carefully package our plants to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Each plant is securely wrapped to prevent soil spillage and damage during transit. We use eco-friendly packaging materials and ship via expedited methods to minimize time in transit. All plants come with care instructions to help you get started.

We offer a 30-day guarantee on all our plants. If your plant arrives damaged or dies within 30 days of delivery, please contact us with photos, and we'll provide a replacement or refund. Please note that this guarantee does not cover plants that die due to improper care or environmental conditions.

Currently, we only ship within the United States. International shipping presents challenges with customs regulations and ensuring plant health during extended transit times. We're working on expanding our shipping capabilities and hope to offer international shipping in the future.

Watering frequency depends on the specific plant, its size, and your home environment. As a general rule, most houseplants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Each plant on our website includes specific care instructions, and all orders come with a care guide. If you're ever unsure, feel free to contact us for personalized advice.

Whether you're troubleshooting an issue with your plant or need advice on creating the perfect plant setup for your space, our team is here to help.
